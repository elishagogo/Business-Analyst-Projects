1. **Increase Customer Retention**
   - Implement loyalty program
   - Send personalized follow-up emails
   - Offer repeat customer discounts

2. **Expand Electronics Category**
   - Add more premium electronic products
   - Create bundles with accessories
   - Target North region for high-value sales

3. **Boost Low-Performing Categories**
   - Promotional campaigns for Books
   - Bundle deals for Home & Garden items
   - Seasonal marketing for Clothing

4. **Regional Marketing**
   - Targeted campaigns in South region
   - Premium product focus in North region
   - Volume-based promotions in East region

### 7.2 Long-term Strategy (3-12 months)
1. **Customer Segmentation**
   - Develop distinct strategies for each segment
   - Create personalized marketing campaigns
   - Build customer profiles for better targeting

2. **Product Portfolio Optimization**

3. **Data Infrastructure**
