## Customer Metrics

### 4. Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
**Definition:** Total revenue expected from a customer  
**Formula:** `(Average Purchase Value 칑 Purchase Frequency) 칑 Average Customer Lifespan`  
**Target:** $2,000  
**Status:** 游늵 To Be Calculated

### 5. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
**Definition:** Cost to acquire a new customer  
**Formula:** `Total Marketing Spend / Number of New Customers`  
**Target:** $50  
**Status:** 游늵 To Be Calculated

### 6. Customer Retention Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of customers retained over time  
**Formula:** `((CE - CN) / CS) 칑 100` where CE=customers at end, CN=new customers, CS=customers at start  
**Target:** 85%  
**Status:** 游늵 To Be Calculated

### 7. Churn Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of customers who stop buying  
**Formula:** `(Customers Lost / Total Customers at Start) 칑 100`  
**Target:** <5%  
**Status:** 游늵 To Be Calculated

**Status:** 游리 Below Target
