# Stakeholder Analysis

## Stakeholder Identification and Assessment

### Primary Stakeholders

#### 1. Executive Team (CEO, CFO, COO)
- **Interest Level:** High
- **Influence Level:** High
- **Requirements:**
  - High-level strategic insights
  - ROI and financial impact
  - Executive dashboard with key metrics
- **Communication:**
  - Monthly executive briefings
  - Quarterly strategic reviews
- **Engagement Strategy:** Keep Satisfied - Regular updates on key metrics and strategic insights

#### 2. Sales Director
- **Interest Level:** High
- **Influence Level:** High
- **Requirements:**
  - Sales performance metrics
  - Regional analysis
  - Team performance data
  - Forecasting insights
- **Communication:**
  - Weekly performance reports
  - Daily dashboard access
- **Engagement Strategy:** Manage Closely - Active collaboration and regular feedback

#### 3. Marketing Manager
- **Interest Level:** High
- **Influence Level:** Medium
- **Requirements:**
  - Customer segmentation data
  - Product performance analysis
  - Campaign effectiveness metrics
  - Market trends
- **Communication:**
  - Bi-weekly meetings
  - Access to analytics platform
- **Engagement Strategy:** Keep Informed - Regular updates and collaboration on campaigns

#### 4. IT Department
- **Interest Level:** Medium
- **Influence Level:** High
- **Requirements:**
  - Technical specifications
  - Data infrastructure requirements
  - Integration requirements
  - Security protocols
- **Communication:**
  - Weekly technical meetings
  - Documentation sharing
- **Engagement Strategy:** Manage Closely - Critical for implementation

### Secondary Stakeholders

#### 5. Finance Team
- **Interest Level:** Medium
- **Influence Level:** Medium
- **Requirements:**
  - Financial reporting
  - Budget vs. actual analysis
  - Revenue forecasting
- **Communication:**
  - Monthly financial reports
- **Engagement Strategy:** Keep Informed

#### 6. Customer Service Team
- **Interest Level:** Medium
- **Influence Level:** Low
- **Requirements:**
  - Customer behavior insights
  - Return/refund patterns
- **Communication:**
  - Quarterly updates
- **Engagement Strategy:** Monitor

#### 7. Operations Team
- **Interest Level:** Medium
- **Influence Level:** Medium
- **Requirements:**
  - Inventory insights
  - Demand forecasting
  - Supply chain optimization data
- **Communication:**
  - Monthly reports
- **Engagement Strategy:** Keep Informed

## Power/Interest Grid

```
High Power, High Interest (Manage Closely):
- Executive Team
- Sales Director
- IT Department

High Power, Low Interest (Keep Satisfied):
- None identified

Low Power, High Interest (Keep Informed):
- Marketing Manager
- Finance Team

Low Power, Low Interest (Monitor):
- Customer Service Team
```

## Communication Plan

| Stakeholder | Frequency | Method | Content |
|-------------|-----------|--------|---------|
| Executive Team | Monthly | Presentation | Strategic insights, KPIs |
| Sales Director | Weekly | Email + Meeting | Performance metrics, trends |
| Marketing Manager | Bi-weekly | Meeting | Customer insights, campaigns |
| IT Department | Weekly | Meeting | Technical updates, issues |
| Finance Team | Monthly | Report | Financial analysis |
| Customer Service | Quarterly | Email | Customer insights |
| Operations Team | Monthly | Report | Demand forecasts |

## Success Factors
- Clear and consistent communication
- Regular stakeholder engagement
- Addressing concerns promptly
- Delivering value early and often
- Managing expectations proactively
